Caponata

Description:

Caponata is a vibrant and hearty Sicilian dish, a rich vegetable medley that celebrates the flavors of the Mediterranean. This beloved Italian classic typically features silky eggplant, sweet bell peppers, juicy tomatoes, and briny olives, all slow-cooked to perfection. The ingredients are caramelized together with a balance of sweet and tangy flavors, achieved by adding vinegar, a touch of sugar, and sometimes raisins or pine nuts.

Fresh basil and capers lend an aromatic brightness, while a finishing drizzle of quality olive oil brings everything together.
