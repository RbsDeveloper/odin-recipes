Sloppy Joe

Description:

A sloppy joe is a warm, savory sandwich made with ground beef cooked in a rich, tangy tomato-based sauce.

The meat is combined with diced onions, bell peppers, and garlic, then simmered with a blend of ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, and spices until thick and flavorful.

Ingredients:

  1. 1 pound lean ground beef
  2. 1/4 cup chopped onion
  3. 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
  4. 3/4 cup ketchup, or to taste
  5. 1 tablespoon brown sugar, or to taste
  6. 1 teaspoon yellow mustard, or to taste
  7. 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  8. salt and ground black pepper to taste
  9. 6 hamburger buns, split
